About Surf's Up

Why Surf's Up

At Surf's Up we love making your hot summer days just a little bit better by working our hardest to make an amazing snowcone. Surf's Up does not operate like a normal resturant regarding the hours, but we also love the feedback regarding our location and our hours. We also love hearing what you guys want when it comes to new flavors to best satisfy your wants and needs. We will try our hardest to make our business convenient to our customers. We are always open to suggestions. We even offer special package deals for birthday parties and special events, please contact us for more information.

We love the oppurtunity to serve our customers and offer them a nice solution to a hot summer day. We are not only a great place to get a nice cool treat in the hot summer, we are also very good at what we do having been rated 4.9 out of 5!!!

Meet Lawson

Lawson Rudd is the owner and operator of Surf's Up. Lawson grew up in Jackson and is now a senior at Jackson Christian School. at 18 years old, Lawson realizes that owning and operating her first business is a great oppurtunity to learn about achieving success in the business world. She will be attending Pepperdine University in Malibu, California where she plans to major in business.
