About Surf's Up

Why Surf's Up

Surf's Up is owned and operated by Lawson Rudd. She lives in Jackson, Tennessee where she is a Senior at Jackson Christian School. She realizes that owning and operating a business at this age is a great opportunity to learn and have success in the business world.

We are not only a great place to get a nice cool treat in the hot summer, we are also very good at what we do having been rated 4.9 out of 5!!!

At Surf's Up we love making your hot summer days just a little bit better by working our hardest to make an amazing snowcone. We love hearing what you guys want when it comes to new flavors to best satisfy your wants and needs. We also love the feedback regarding our location and our hours. We are always open to suggestions.
