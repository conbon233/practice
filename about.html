About Surf's Up

How We Operate

At Surf's Up, we love making your hot summer days just a little bit better by working our hardest to make an amazing snowcone.

Our truck is most often located at 3100 N Highland in Jackson. We operate at this location with regular hours from Memorial Day through Labor Day and by request during other months. Normal Surf's Up hours through the summer are:
Monday-Thursday: 11:00 a.m. - 8:00 p.m.
Friday-Saturday: 10:00 a.m. - 9:00 p.m.
Sunday: CLOSED except by special request

We offer package deals for birthday parties and other special occasions. Let us give you a price for your special event.

Meet Lawson

Lawson Rudd is the owner and operator of Surf's Up. Lawson grew up in Jackson and is now a senior at Jackson Christian School. At 18 years old, Lawson realizes that owning and operating her first business is a great opportunity to get experience and learn about achieving success in the business world. After graduation from high school, she will be attending Pepperdine University in Malibu, California where she plans to major in business.
